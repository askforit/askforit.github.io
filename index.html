Market

Problem

Existing delivery platforms have constraints

The existing peer to peer delivery platforms have the constraint of only delivering food and drinks from restaurants or big food chains.
We think that constraint creates a gap where other items such as a coffee or even our personal items that we forgot somewhere are also deliverable objects and that's what we bring new to the customers besides the existing food delivering from restaurants.

Solution

AskForIt helps customers to solve their expectation by asking to other people to bring them their own need that is better than Uber Eats, for example, which take much more time and deals only with professional (restaurants, bars….).

To reduce the main problem, the creation of an app can be helpful where users are able to:

Get everything they want without moving

Solve customer expectation by asking to other people to bring them their own need.

Earn money
easily

Earn money by working for the app and bring the expectation of the buyer.

Competition


We are the first company to create an app that people can solve their all-days expectation without paying an extravagant price.
Of course, it exists company such as uber eats, Deliveroo, allo resto, ect...
But we can´t compare our app to this business as it takes much more time and deals only with professional (restaurants, bars…)

Business Model

The canvas of AskForIt offers a justification of what the company uses in delivering and enhancing the values of its service. The canvas of this business model serves as the strategy that is implemented through the frameworks, systems and processes of this company.

Key
Partners

The key partners Askforit are Students and cafeteria from the university of Catolica.

In The
beggining

We will start by spreading awareness of our app via social media, digital marketing and direct advertising through the university as we aim to market our app in Catolica first.

Revenue Stream

Through the app, the buyer pays for the product plus 15% of its cost and 10% are kept by the person that did it and the app get 5%.
